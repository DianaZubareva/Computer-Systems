Modern Computers

A device that computes, especially a programmable electronic machine that performs high-speed mathematical or logical operations or that assembles, stores, correlates, or otherwise processes information

Modern computers are programable, digital & electronic.

  • Programmable - capable of being programmed for automatic operation or computer processing.
  • Digital - expressed as series of the digits 0 and 1, typically represented by values of a physical quantity such as voltage or magnetic polarization.
  • Electronic - using, based on, or used in a system of operation that involves the control of electric current by various devices.
